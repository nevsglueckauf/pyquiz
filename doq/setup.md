<kbd>git clone https://github.com/nevsglueckauf/pyquiz </kbd>

<kbd>cd pyquiz</kbd>



## VENV

<code>sven@Thanos pyquiz % </code><kbd>python3 -m venv .venv</kbd>

<code>sven@Thanos pyquiz % </code><kbd>source .venv/bin/activate</kbd>
